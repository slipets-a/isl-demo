How it works

Our Video Relay Service enables Deaf and Hard of Hearing individuals to reach out to their hearing peers in real time via a qualified British Sign Language Interpreter - using our technology. Our App can be downloaded on both IOS and Android devices. All you need is a reliable internet connection and you’re good to go!
